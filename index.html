About Davangere Benne Dosa

Davangere benne dose (or butter dosa) is a type of dosa that traces its origin to the city of Davanagere in Karnataka, India. The term "benne dose" in Kannada simply means "butter dosa." It is prepared by adding a generous amount of butter while cooking, and is accompanied by coconut chutney. Its batter is unique, made from rice, dal, puffed rice, and other ingredients, traditionally prepared on a wood-fired pan. Unlike masala dosa or set dosa, it is smaller in size, but rich in taste with more butter. It is always served hot with liberal helpings of butter sprinkled on top.
