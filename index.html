About Davangere Benne Dosa

Davangere Benne Dose is a special type of dosa from Davanagere, Karnataka, known for its crispy texture and generous use of butter- "benne" means butter in Kannada. This smaller-than-usual dosa is made from a unique batter of rice, lentils, and puffed rice, cooked traditionally on a wood-fired pan.
